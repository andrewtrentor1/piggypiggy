📋 RULES AND REGULATIONS 📋

  • 🗳️ Majority Rules!
  • 🎯 All players start with 30 pts
  • 🚫 No gambling money allowed!
  • 💰 Anyone caught trying to gamble forfeits 1 pt to God
  • 🏌️‍♂️ Any hole in one results in an auto MVP
  • 📈 Double bogeys pay 1 pt to each player that wins the hole
  • 🐦 Birdies: God pays 1 pt per player
  • 🦅 Eagles: God pays 2 pts per player
  • 🚫 If a player can't golf they forfeit double the max pts they could lose - Half to God, half to their partner
  • ⛳ The solo golfer gets 1 God shot per hole
  • 🏆 Forgetting to bring either trophy is -5 pts
  • 📅 Must present trophy within two weeks
  • ⚠️ Failing to comply you start next MBE with down half pts
  • 📉 Longer than a month start at 0 pts
  • 🐷 If a player gets the pig trophy 3 years in a row they must wear a pig costume/clothes during all golf events
  • ⚠️ Danger Zone: 5 dice
    • Under 18: you take shot
    • 18-22: everyone shot
    • Over 23: everyone but you shot
  • 🏌️ Golf Day 1: 1st: +15 | 2nd: -5 | 3rd: -10
  • 🏌️ Golf Day 2: 1st: +25 | 2nd: -10 | 3rd: -15
  • 🎯 All par 3s are CTP - Winner: +5 | Losers: -1 each
